Stars · astro-ph.SR
Magnetic activity cycles in solar-type stars across the main sequence
A new survey of 847 FGK dwarfs finds that magnetic cycle periods scale with rotation, with a secondary population of flat-activity stars that may be in a Maunder-like minimum.
Lindqvist et al. · Apr 2025 · Read on arXiv →
Exoplanets · astro-ph.EP
Atmospheric escape rates in sub-Neptune planets orbiting M dwarfs
Using UV transit observations, the authors measure helium outflow in six sub-Neptunes and find that XUV flux dominates over stellar wind in driving mass loss at short periods.
Sørensen & Park et al. · Apr 2025 · Read on arXiv →
